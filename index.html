Home Storage Settings and Help

Hey! Welcome to INSERT APP NAME. You store and manage items longterm by telling her in the command line. It’s really that simple. Now I know that you obviously won't use this for little things, but maybe for those things that are worth your time to put it into the app. Things that you use once a year, or once in a while, or just things that you often misplace.

The app does remind you if you haven’t used an item in over a year (this can be adjusted in the settings), this way, you’ll be able to maybe get rid of some stuff that you don’t actually need but thought you needed.

Type in a phrase into the command line on the home page.

Examples of how to store stuff are:

Pencil case on shelf
Weedwacker in shed
I put my smash controllers in the attic

Some examples to move stuff from one place to another are:

Move old laptop case to closet shelf
I moved my Purple shampoo under my sink

You can also find items with these phrases:

Where is my rubik's cube
Where are the extra bars of soap

These instructions can be accessed from the "i" button on the top right corner. The command input is also fairly intuitive, meaning there are other ways to access these three commands.

Hope this app helps!
