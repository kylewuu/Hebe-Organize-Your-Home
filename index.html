Hey! Welcome to INSERT APP NAME. You store and manage items longterm by telling her in the command line. It’s really that simple. Now I know that you obviously won't use this for little things, but maybe for those things that are worth your time to put it into the app. Things that you use once a year, or once in a while, or just things that you always misplace.

The app does remind you if you haven’t used an item in over a year (this can be adjusted in the settings), this way, you’ll be able to maybe get rid of some stuff that you don’t actually need but thought you needed.

Type in a phrase into the command line on the home page.

The keyword to store stuff is:
Item in location

An example way to move stuff from one place to another is:
Move item to new location

You can also find items with the keywords:
Where is my item

These instructions can be accessed from the "i" button on the top right corner. The command input is also fairly intuitive, meaning there are other ways to access these three commands.

Hope this app helps!
